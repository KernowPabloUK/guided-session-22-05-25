Why Visit Us

Cornwall offers breathtaking coastal scenery, charming villages, and a rich cultural heritage. Enjoy stunning beaches, delicious local cuisine, and unique attractions like historic castles and gardens. Whether you love outdoor adventures or relaxing by the sea, Cornwall is the perfect destination for an unforgettable holiday.

Transport / How to get to Cornwall

Travel to Cornwall by train, car, coach, or plane. The Great Western Railway connects London and Cornwall, while Newquay Airport offers domestic flights. Major roads like the A30 provide easy car access.

Attractions / Events

Discover the Eden Project, St Michael’s Mount, and Tintagel Castle. Enjoy local festivals, music events, and traditional Cornish celebrations throughout the year.

Beach / Surf

Cornwall boasts world-famous beaches like Fistral and Porthcurno. Surf the Atlantic waves, relax on golden sands, or explore hidden coves along the stunning coastline.

Accommodation

Choose from cosy cottages, seaside hotels, family-friendly campsites, or luxury resorts. There’s something for every taste and budget in Cornwall.

Cuisine

Savour Cornish pasties, fresh seafood, and cream teas. Enjoy local produce at farmers’ markets and dine at award-winning restaurants across the region.
